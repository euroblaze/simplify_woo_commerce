What does the odoo-WooCommerce interface do?

Are you using Odoo for your day to day operations, and Wordpress for online-sale? Woo provides a simple database structure and a variety of community plugins. Our odoo-WooCommerce interface enables the automated and manual exchange of shop data into ERP for further processing:

  1. Product & categories
  2. Customers
  3. Orders
  4. Taxes
  5. Manage stocks
  6. Manage prices
  7. Connect multiple Woocommerce stores to Odoo

Product management and category management

With the odoo-WooCommerce interface, you as a shop owner can handle all product management tasks, such as: price adjustments, variant maintenance, image management and more in odoo centralized done.

  1. Bidirectionally downloadable prices, article descriptions and stocks
  2. Real-time synchronization of prices and inventory data
  3. Export products and products images
  4. Search and filter products in various criteria

Customer and data management

Customer master data can also be managed in odoo. The interface groups customer data by sales channel. All orders as well as all communication to individual orders are to be found under the customer files.

  1. Periodic auto-backup of customer data
  2. Instant synchronization of changes in customer data
  3. Segmentation of customers by country, tags, sales channel and more
  4. Search and filter customers in various criteria

Order processing

WooCommerce orders can be processed centrally in odoo. Invoice and shipping documents can then be created uniformly in odoo. Tax amounts are also separated from the gross order amount and stored in dedicated accounting accounts.

  1. One-click invoice creation
  2. Send email of shipping/tracking-number with ready-made templates
  3. Central processing of orders
  4. Grouping orders by customers to your preference
  5. Search and filter orders in various criteria

Stocks are updated bidirectionally


If the stocks in Woocommerce change because of the sale of products, they are automatically mirrored in odoo via the odoo-Woocommerce interface. Likewise, stock changes in odoo are automatically uploaded to the Woocommerce Shop.

Updating the prices


Do you want to adjust the prices for a specific product? You can change this in odoo, and upload them to Woocommerce right away.

The product prices are manageable in Odoo.


You can manage and set the prices individually for the article. Different prices can be set for different variants that belong to the same article.

Product search according to various criteria.


In order to be able to work in odoo as efficiently as possible, it is possible to search for products using various product attributes or metadata.

Export products in Woocommerce


Do you have a major update from your catalog. You can enter them directly in odoo and update them in Woocommerce at the click of a button.

Management of product media files


As a webshop operator, you definitely want to attract customers with nice pictures. The images can be entered in odoo, and uploaded individually per product or the entire catalog.

Remove items from the Woocommerce workshop


If you do not want to have certain items in your Woocommerce workshop, you can remove them from odoo at the click of a button. The same procedure applies to removing a particular variant article.

How do I install this module?

This module can be installed like any other module.
Download the zip file, and extract it to your preffered addons folder. Once there update the apps list in youre Apps section, search for the technical name of this module and click on install.

Important Note: This module is depended on the OKM Core module. Before installing, you'll also need to download and extract the OKM Core into the same or any other addons folder.
